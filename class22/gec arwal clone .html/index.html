GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE (GEC), Arwal is one of the new Government Engineering College established by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science, Technology and Technical Education. It started its first academic session in 2019. The new Engineering College has five Engineering Branches: Civil Engineering intake 60, Computer Science & Engineering intake 60, Computer Science & Engineering (Data Science) intake 60, Electrical Engineering intake 120, Mechanical Engineering intake 30, and Robotics & Automation intake 30 and 360 students in total.
